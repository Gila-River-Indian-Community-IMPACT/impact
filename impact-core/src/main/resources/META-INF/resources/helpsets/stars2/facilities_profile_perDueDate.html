PER Due Dates

Overview 

The system’s PER Due Dates page, available from the third-level menu of the Facilities tab, allows you to view a summary datagrid of the due date (current and historical, if applicable) for the facility’s Permit Evaluation Report (PER).  PERs are applicable to sources issued a PTIO or FEPTIO and this table will only identify the PER due date if one of these permits has been issued final.  There is also the ability to update a PER due date (change it).  However, this is a function that will be handled by Central Office.  If a facility is requesting a change to their PER due date contact the Central Office permit issuance section.

Printable View and Exporting to Excel

Information from the PER Due Dates list may be viewed, exported to Excel or printed. By clickingyou may view and print. By clickinga pop-up will appear in which you must click Open in order to view the information in Excel.

Terms and Conditions

Overview

The Terms and Conditions third-level menu allows you to generate, view, delete and edit versions of Terms and Conditions for a specific permit that is being viewed through the Permit Detail page. However, if the permit has been issued final the Terms and Conditions document(s) will only be viewable.

The header at the top of the Terms and Conditions page is the same header viewed on the Permit Detail page and identifies specific information regarding the permit; most importantly the Permit Number, Permit Type and issuance dates for various drafts, public notices, comment periods, effective dates and expiration dates, when applicable. Under the header is a datagrid that lists all versions of Terms and Conditions associated with the permit action.

Generating the shell permit and making changes

If Terms and Conditions have not been generated, or an alternate version needs created, this can be accomplished by clicking on the Edit button and select the Generate button. A pop-up page will appear. Enter a Description for the file as you wish it to appear in the datagrid and click on the Apply button to continue to Generate your shell permit document. If you do not wish to continue you may click on the Cancel button.

Once you have clicked on the Apply button the pop-up window will be dismissed and you will return to the Terms and Conditions page. If you decide that you were not ready to Generate the shell permit yet, or if you opened it immediately and now want to remove it from the datagrid, you can delete it by clicking the Discard Changes button. Otherwise, click Save Changes.

In order to open the permit document so you can develop the Terms and Conditions, double-click on the document’s name as it appears under the Description column of the datagrid. Once the document has been opened, it is independent of the system and may be saved to your hard/network drive and edited as you wish. It must then be uploaded to be available in the system. See below for help with uploading.

Uploading the Terms and Conditions document to the System

Terms and Conditions are developed in Microsoft Word 2007. After developing the Terms and Conditions, you need to attach the Word document containing your work to the Terms and Conditions page. Click on the Edit button and then select the Upload button. This opens a pop-up window asking you to enter a Description of the file you are uploading, which will appear in the list of Terms and Conditions. You may browse for the file to Upload. Click on the Browse button to locate the Terms and Conditions document that you wish to attach. To complete attaching the Terms and Conditions that you created click on the Apply button. You will be returned to the Terms and Conditions page. The file you Upload will be added to the top of the data grid. If you decide you do not want the file attached you can delete it by clicking the Discard Changes button, otherwise to save your work click on the Save Changes button.

Designating Terms and Conditions that should be issued

Above the data grid is the statement Move the version you would like to have issued to the top of the list. The top file in the Terms and Conditions datagrid will be used by the system for issuance; therefore, any viewer will also assume that the top entry is the most current. You should always move the file that is the most current to the top of the list. To do this click the Top button next to the appropriate file. To save your work click on the Save Changes button.

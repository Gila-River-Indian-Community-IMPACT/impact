Invoice History

Overview 

The system’s Invoice History page, of the Facility Profile page allows you to view a list of all invoices associated with the facility. This page also allows you to access a specific Invoice Detail page, through a hyperlink for each invoice.  Viewing invoices is discussed on the first-level menu Invoice Detail Help page.   After DO/LAA staff have completed review of the object requiring invoicing and signed off on the workflow, Central Office staff will begin the invoicing process. This page can be viewed by staff to find the status of all invoicing for a particular facility.

Invoice History List

The following datagrid of information will appear when you access the third-level menu, Invoice History:


  • Invoice Id – The unique invoice identification number that was assigned to the individual invoice.  A hyperlink for accessing the specific Invoice Detail page.
  • Revenue Id – The unique revenue identification number that was assigned to the individual revenue from the invoice.
  • Revenue Type – Unique coding used by Central Office staff for categorizing revenues.
  • Report/Permit Id – A hyperlinked unique identification number generated by the system that will take you to the specific object associated with the invoice; for example the Permit Detail page for a PTI or PTIO that was issued or the Emissions Report Detail page for emissions reporting.
  • Stars2 Invoice State – Identifies the processing status of the invoice; for example, Posted to Revenues or Ready to Invoice. 
  • Revenue State – Identifies the status of the revenue from the invoice; for example, Paid in Full or Certified to AGO. 
  • Invoice Date – Date selected by invoicer to be printed on invoice document.
  • Due Date – Date payment is due to Ohio EPA to avoid interest penalty assessment.
  • Original Amount – Amount due to Ohio EPA at time of invoice creation and/or prior invoice.
  • Current Amount – The invoice amount when the invoice was initially created and posted to REVENUES.

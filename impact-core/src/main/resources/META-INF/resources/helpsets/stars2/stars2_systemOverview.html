System Overview

This system provides straight forward web-based access to the functionality you require to support your work for the Ohio EPA's Division of Air Pollution Control. It supports the full range of permitting activity, maintenance of Facility information, Emissions report processing, including invoicing, and Compliance report processing. A subset of these capabilities is available to Regulated Community Users (RCUs) via the IMPACT System that RCU's may access via the Ohio EPA eBusiness Gateway. There, RCUs may see AQD's view of their facility's history (permitting, reporting, etc.), make changes to their Facility Detail, and enter permitting requests and reports via web-based forms similar to those available on this system to AQD personnel.

The system brings all the information about each facility together and makes it available from a single location so that you can access whatever information you need when you need it. The system also provides reports and tools that give managers and adminstrators additional information they can use to effectively support you in your work.

In addition to an overview of the system's organization, this help topic also gives you a brief summary of the functionality you will find under each tab, as well as links to help topics that provide more detail on that functionality and how you can use it effectively.

System Organization

The system is organized around the basic services that you provide to each other, to the Agency, the State, and to your clients, the regulated facilities throughout the state:

  • Manage your tasks
  • Manage the workflow for AQD services such as permit and report processing
  • Organize and access current and historical data about the Facilities with which you interact
  • Process permit applications (and permitting action requests)
  • Develop air permits
  • Process submitted emissions inventories
  • Process submitted compliance reports
  • Manage permitting and emissions fee invoicing
  • Provide helpful management reports, along with an infrastructure to create new reports as needed
  • Provide tools to support various functionality, including document generation, correspondence management, facility-based event tracking, facility role management,
  • Administer aspects of the system to accommodate changing information and changing needs

You will only be able to see and access the information and capabilities that are associated with the 'role' you are assigned in the system. Therefore, you may not be able to see or use some of the functionality described below.

The remainder of this page gives you an overview of the capability you can access from the tabs, each of which represents one of the organizational areas listed above. In addition, you will find links to Related Help Topics at the bottom of the page.

Home Tab

The Home tab keeps track of all the active work items in the system. By default, it shows all the 'In Process' tasks assigned to you; it is your To Do list. The list gives you the mechanism to manage your workload. It has information about each task, including how long tasks have been In Process and their status, for example, 'Jeopardy' or 'Late'. It also has links to information about the facility associated with the task, how the individual task fits and where it falls within the workflow of which it is a step. Linked task screens provide you support with respect to completing the task.

You may also explicitly add work items to your To Do list --- an effective mechanism to keep track of all you have to do. Managers may also use the list to track the workload of their staff members, as well as the status of work related to specific facilities.

Workflow Tab

The Workflow tab allows you to locate In Process workflows in the system.  A workflow tracks the various activities and tasks that take place in order to accomplish a particular processing operation. For example, taking a permit application and following it through all the steps until it has been "Issued Final". The Workflow Diagram offers you a pictorial representation of the step and allows you to see, at a glance, where the its life cycle. If you have appropriate authority, you can manipulate, from this tab, the tasks in the workflow --- Re-assign them to a different staff member, Refer them to an outside agency, or back to the applicant, etc. For tasks assigned to you, you can notify the system that you have completed the task, so that it moves the workflow to its next task.

Also under this tab, you can see charts depicting summary information you might find interesting, such as Open Workflows Processes by Status (OK, Jeopardy, Late) or by Type (Permitting, Emissions Reporting, etc.) The summary charts give you capability to "drill down" to the level of the individual workflows for any status or type you choose.

Facilities Tab

The Facilities tab lets you locate individual facilities in the system using various combinations of search criteria such as supporting DO/LAA, County, Facility Class, Permit Status, etc. Upon selecting an individual facility, this tab provides an overview of the facility by displaying the Facility Detail. The Profile is the heart of the Stars2 system, because it displays or offers direct links to all the current and historical information about that facility, from how its control equipment is connected to any undelivered mail the division has had for the facility. In addition to information access, if you are authorized to do so, you can manipulate facility information such as Operational Status, Facility Class, Emissions Reporting Category, etc. Nodes in the facility tree give you direct links to detail data about each emissions unit, emissions process, instance of control equipment and release point that make up a facility's Emissions Profile.

Applications Tab

The Applications tab allows you to locate any electronic version of an application for a specific facility by applying search criteria. Upon selecting the specific search criteria, a datagrid will be displayed with all applications that meet the criteria. Each of these applications can be viewed by clicking on the Application “Request Number” on the far left of the datagrid.

Applications that have not been submitted to the system can be manipulated/edited in preparation of final submission. Applications that have been previously submitted are not editable but information contained in the application can be copied into new application or used again for subsequent application preparation. Duplicative entry of data is therefore reduced to a minimum.

Permits Tab

The Permits tab houses all of the electronic versions of permits ever issued, denied, pending, or in any stage of issuance. Using search criteria, you can find permits and pending permit actions specifically for each facility in the database. Permits that were issued prior to the June 30th, 2008 date are available in either HTML or WordPerfect formats. These permits are attached to each permitting action in either the attachments section or in the terms and conditions sections of the permit detail page.

Permit actions that have not been completed can be searched for and manipulated. Permit actions that have been finalized and issued are available for download, but the final versions of the permits are not editable.

Emissions Inventories Tab

The Emissions Inventories tab allows you to search for general and specific emissions inventories that are either in progress, need reviewed, or have been approved and subsequently invoiced. The reports that have not been submitted will be viewable and editable, but those reports that have been submitted and subsequently approved will only be available for versioning/copying and editing as needed.

Back to Top

Compliance Reports Tab

The Compliance Reports tab allows you to search for compliance reports associated with specific facilities or those related to a specific DO/LAA or the entire State of Ohio. Each search criteria field can be filled in to limit the search to as narrow or broad a spectrum you wish. The reports that are currently required to be submitted electronically from TV and SMTV facilities will be searchable through this tab. These include TVCC and PERs for FEPTIOs. Other compliance reports may or may not be available through the compliance report tab depending on DO/LAA and external client participation. Each compliance report that is added to a specific facility to the Compliance Report(s) third level menu will be available in the future.

It is important to note that no tracking mechanism has been established to track compliance with report submission through this program.

Invoices Tab

For a limited number of users, the Invoices tab provides the capability to post invoices to Revenues and to Print Invoices in bulk operations. In addition, it allows you to search for invoices according to several important criteria, such as and to view individual invoice details. Although anyone with access to this tab can see most invoice details, in order to see details reflecting activities in Revenues, such as whether the invoice has been paid, and the current balance on the invoice, the system must be able to communicate with the Revenue system on your behalf.

Management Reports Tab

This tab provides management with several reports that they can use to manage their work, and yours. In addition, authorized users may insert reports of a specific type into the system. You may develop such reports outside the system with a very flexible third-party tool. After reports have been added to the Report Catalog, the reports can be run, on demand from this tab.

Back to Top

Tools Tab

The Tools Tab gives you the capability to perform certain general functions and bulk operations without having to involve a system administrator. For example, in this tab you can search the log of events that the system records, such as changes in the value of selected data items and you can filter your search to acccess only events that apply to a particular facility or group of facilities, or those related to permitting activity or events that were recorded during a time period you can specify. Some internal logs and other operations may be available to selected users.

You can do similar searches on the Correspondence and Correspondence history that the system maintains, including tracking certified mail. This tab also provides you with direct links to lots of websites that can easily provide you with important reference data and other guidance, as well as access to some temporary files, such as documents you generate that aren't stored in the system, for a short period of time before they are purged.

Back to Top

Admin Tab

The Admin Tab provides an interface to the system for a very small group of users who have been assigned adminstrative access to the system. Most users will never see this tab. Administrators can access and modify reference data, adjust the system roles assigned to user (which control to some extent what each user can see and do in the system) and perform other rarely-required administrative functions. There are no Help Topics available for this administrative functionality. Instead, these capabilities are documented in the Stars2 System Administrator's Guide.

Invoice Detail

Overview

The Invoice Detail page provides invoicing information for sources subject to invoicing, for example invoicing from fee emissions reporting or the issuance of a permit-to-install (PTI) or permit-to-install and operate (PTIO) . After DO/LAA staff have completed review of the object requiring invoicing and signed off on the workflow, Central Office staff will begin the invoicing process. This page can be viewed by staff to find the status of invoicing for a particular facility’s invoice. Editing and entry of the information in this page will not be performed by DO/LAA staff.

Locating and Viewing an Invoice

You can locate an invoice and access an Invoice Detail page throughout many of the system pages, most usually through the Invoice Search page. Invoice Search allows you to search for available invoices using many different search criteria. This feature is available on the opening page for the first-level menu called Invoices. For Help on this feature, refer to the Invoice Search Help page. Invoices that fit the search criteria you have set will show up in the Invoice List datagrid after you have performed an Invoice Search. The datagrid will contain a column with the Invoice Id which is hyperlinked to the specific Invoice Detail page. Click on the Invoice Id.

The datagrid will also contain a column named Report/Permit Id. Clicking on the hyperlinked Report/Permit Id will take you to the specific object associated with the invoice; for example the Permit Detail page for a PTI or PTIO that was issued or the Emissions Inventory Detail page for emissions reporting.

Invoice Detail Data Fields

The information you see on the Invoice Detail page may depend on the type of invoice you are viewing. At the top of the page is basic information about the invoice, including the Revenue State (e.g., Paid in Full). The following identifies the information in the major sections of the Invoice Detail page.

Billing Details

  • Posted Date- Date the invoice amount is generated in REVENUES by invoicer.
  • Due Date- Date payment is due to Ohio EPA to avoid interest penalty assessment.
  • Invoice Date- Date selected by invoicer to be printed on invoice document.
  • Created Date- For Permitting invoices, the Permit Issuance Date is both the ‘Created’ date in the invoice record and the Effective date in REVENUES. For Emissions Inventory invoices, ‘Created’ date is the date when the system ‘created’ an invoice record, while Effective date is the date the user enters when ‘Post’ing to REVENUES.
  • Report/Permit Id- A hyperlinked unique identification number generated by the system that will take you to the specific object associated with the invoice; for example the Permit Detail page for a PTI or PTIO that was issued or the Emissions Inventory Detail page for emissions reporting.
  • Revenues Original Amount- Amount due to Ohio EPA at time of invoice creation and/or prior invoice.
  • Revenues Current Amount- Total Amount billed to this facility on this invoice, as reported by REVENUES.
  • Balance Due- The remaining balance of the invoice to be paid by the facility. If $0.00, indicates paid in full.
  • Current Invoice Total Amount- The invoice amount when the invoice was initially created and posted to REVENUES.
  • Print Invoice- Once the generate document button has been clicked, the user will see document hyperlink next to this print invoice text. This will allow user to re-print an invoice at any point in time.
  • Billing Contact Information- The name, address and phone information for the person at the facility receiving the billing information.

Emissions Inventory Details

Only applicable to invoices resulting for emissions inventories. This section displays a datagrid of the facility level emissions that were reported for the given year(s) that are subject to fees. To view the details of the emissions inventory, use the hyperlinked Report/Permit Id to access the Emissions Inventory Detail page.

Adjustments

This section contains a datagrid that will identify any adjustments associated with the invoice. It will also identify by date Payment Made when a payment was received.

Back to Top

Notes

This section contains a datagrid of Notes entered by Central Office regarding the invoice. The full text of the note can be viewed by clicking on the Note Id hyperlink.

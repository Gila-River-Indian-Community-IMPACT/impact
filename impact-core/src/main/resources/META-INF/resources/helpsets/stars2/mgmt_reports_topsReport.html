TOPS Report

The system’s second-level menu, TOPS Report, of the Mgmt Reports tab allows you to view the data that Ohio EPA passes to U.S. EPA on a semi-annual basis. The information is used to feed the federal Title V Operating Permits System (TOPS). TOPS is used by U.S. EPA to show federal Title V program oversight efficiency as part of the federal Office of Management & Budget Performance Assessment Rating Tool (PART) assessing Title V program implementation across the country.

Reporting Period Selection

The report covers a six month period preceding the Report Date that you select.

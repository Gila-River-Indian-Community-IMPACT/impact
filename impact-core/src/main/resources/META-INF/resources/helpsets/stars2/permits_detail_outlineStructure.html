Outline Structure

The permit structure that is used under the new system differs in structure than those used by AQD prior to the deployment of this system.

This new structure is universal for all permit types (e.g., Title V, PTI, PTIO)

The following table illustrates the differences between the previous permit structure and the new permit structure implemented with the system.

Old Permit Designation
New Permit Designation
General Terms and Conditions. Part I. A.
Facility Wide Terms and Conditions. Part II. B.
Emissions Unit Terms and Conditions. Part III. C.

All emissions units heading designations in the old permit structure will remain in the new permit structure (i.e., Operational Restrictions, Testing Requirements, etc.).

Each emissions unit (or grouping of emission units) included in the permit will be listed alphabetically/numerically and have a sequential number designation. (For example; F001-Roadways and Parking Areas will be under C.1.a) through g); F002- Storage Piles will be under C.2.a) through g); and P901- 200 TPH Asphalt Plant will be under C.3.a) through g)).

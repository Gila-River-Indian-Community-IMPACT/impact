Emissions Inventories Summary Information

Overview 

The system’s third-level menu, Emissions Inventories, of the Facility Profile page allows you to view a list of all emissions inventories that have been submitted by the facility.  This page also allows you to access a specific Emissions Report Detail page, through a hyperlink for each emissions inventory, where you can view the emissions inventory information as submitted and also review the emissions report.  Viewing and reviewing emissions reports is discussed on the first-level menu Emissions Reports Help page.

From this page, you can also generate a new emissions report, or access previously submitted emissions inventories in order to revise a report.  This would be necessary in order to enter a hard copy report that has been submitted by the facility.  Please keep in mind, Title V and synthetic minor Title V facilities will be required to submit emissions inventories electronically.  Non-Title V sources may choose to submit an electronic copy or hard copy.  However, Ohio EPA Central Office will be entering all hard copy non-Title V emissions inventories.  Therefore it should not be necessary for DO/LAA staff to enter emissions inventories although the feature is available in this system.  This Help page will not discuss how to create a new or revised emissions inventory.  This page also allows you to access the specific version of the Facility Profile that was applicable at the time the emissions report was submitted. For more information about Facility Profile versions view the Facility Profile History Help page.

Emissions Report(s) List

The following datagrid of information will appear when you access the third-level menu, Emissions Report(s):


  • Report - The unique identification number assigned to the report by Ohio EPA upon submittal. This is a hyperlink to view the Report.
  • Previous Report –  Only available when the Report identified in this row was a revised report submitted to Ohio EPA.  The Previous Report number will be the unique identification number assigned, by Ohio EPA upon submittal, to the report which is being revised.   This is a hyperlink to view the Previous Report.
  • FP Id - The unique identification number assigned to the version of the Facility Detail associated with the Report submitted to Ohio EPA.   This is a hyperlink to view that version of the Facility Profile.
  • DO/LAA- Name of District Office or Local Area Agency, for example, Ohio EPA AQD, Southwest District Office, that the Report was submitted to.
  • Year – Identifies the reporting year the Report was submitted for.
  • Category- Identifies the emissions reporting category that the facility was subject to for that Year; SMTV (synthetic minor Title V), TV (Title V) or NTV (non-Title V).
  • Reporting State – Identifies that status of the Report once submitted; for example, Submitted or Approved. 
  • EIS State – Identifies the status of the emissions inventory system (EIS) portion of the Report, when EIS reporting was applicable. The EIS portion of an emissions inventory can be reviewed and approved separately from other portions of the emissions inventory by Ohio EPA.  For example, Submitted or Approved.  If identified as none, indicates EIS was not applicable for reporting during that year.
  • Received Date – Identifies the date the Report was received by Ohio EPA.
  • FER Emissions
    • Range – Identifies the Range of emissions reported that are subject to fees for a Year, when providing a Range was applicable.  For example, 0 to 50.
    • TONs - Identifies the total TONs of emissions reported that are subject to fees for a Year, when providing TONs was applicable.

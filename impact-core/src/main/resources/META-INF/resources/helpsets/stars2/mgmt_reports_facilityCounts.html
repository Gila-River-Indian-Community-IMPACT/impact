Facility Counts

The system’s second-level menu, Facility Counts, of the Mgmt Reports tab allows you to generate a report of the number of facilities within the entire state with active, expired or extended permits. Extended permits are those permits that have expired and the owner or operator has submitted a timely renewal application but Ohio EPA has not yet issued the renewal permit.

You may search for Facility Counts up to any specified date by entering a To Date under the Facility – Permit Count Report Filter heading and clicking . If you decide you want to change your selections and start over, you can click  to clear the dates. After you enter the dates and click on , the system searches all of the records for facilities with active, expired or extended permits stored in its database, selects the ones up to the date you have specified, and returns summary information about the Facility Counts in a datagrid. The summary data returned is organized by Permit Type (Title V Permit, State PTO, PTI and PTIO) with the number of facilities with active, expired and extended permits displayed for each Permit Type.

As with all datagrids, information from the list may be viewed, exported to Excel or printed. By clicking  you may view and print. By clicking  a pop-up will appear in which you must click Open in order to view the information in Excel.

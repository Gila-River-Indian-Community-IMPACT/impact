Fee Summary

Overview

The Fee Summary third-level menu of the Permit Detail page summarizes the permit-to-install fees that will be billed to the owner or operator of a facility upon final issuance of a PTI or PTIO (for initial installation or modification). Once Ohio EPA has entered fee information for each emissions unit (via the Facility Tree on the Permit Detail page) the Fee Summary page will be automatically populated and display a datagrid, EU Fees, containing all the emissions units included in the permit action and the standard fee associated with each emissions unit.

Some fields of the Fee Summary page may be editable by clicking on the Edit button at the bottom of the page. These features, along with general information about the contents of each field are discussed below.

The following topics are included in this help page:

  • Full Cost Recovery
  • EU Fees Datagrid
  • Fee Totals and Invoice Information
  • Related Help Topics

Full Cost Recovery

At the top of the page is a check box titled Full Cost Recovery. This indicates the permit fee will be based on an alternative calculation (e.g., per hour) rather than the customary standard fee. If this box is not checked and it is decided the permit fee will be established using this method, click the Edit button and select the box by placing a check mark. You can enter an amount into the Other Adjustment field at this time, or it can be entered at a later date. The EU Fees datagrid will still display the customary standard fee for each emissions unit; however, they will not be included in the Total Amount found underneath the datagrid.

EU Fees Datagrid

The datagrid contains the following columns of information:

  • EU id – identifies all the emissions units in the permit.

  • Category – identifies the fee category that the emissions unit, as selected under the Emissions Unit page (via the Facility Tree on the Permit Detail page).

  • Fee name - the power or process weight rate levels associated with a Category type.

  • Base amount - this is the designated fee charged to the owner or operator of a facility based on the Category and Fee Name prior to any adjustments made to the fee.

  • Adjustment – designates any alterations to the Base Amount, for example Half for an Administrative Modification.

  • Adjusted amount - the amount that the owner or operator of a facility will be billed upon final issuance of the PTI or PTIO (except when Full Cost Recovery is selected as discussed above).

Fee Totals and Invoice Information

Below the datagrid you will find the following information:

  • Other adjustments – identifies any additional amounts to be added or deducted from the total fee. You can edit this field by clicking the Edit button and entering a positive or negative number in the field.

  • Total Amount –the calculated sum of all fees and adjustments and is not directly editable. If this number is incorrect, make corrections to the appropriate emissions unit fee assessments and/or enter the proper Other adjustment.

  • Invoice Number –the number of the invoice for this PTI or PTIO, assigned by the system once the owner or operator has been invoiced.

  • Invoice Amount – the total fee assessed by the associated Invoice Number.
